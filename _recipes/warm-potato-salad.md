tags: side

ingredients:
- 6 bacon rashers
- 1 onion
- 8 large potatoes
- 1 or 2 cups of frozen peas
- Add cream

directions:
- Boil potatoes (with pinch of salt in water to add to taste)
- Fry bacon and onion
- Add boiled potatoes (to brown)
- Add peas to cook
- Add cream and stir through
- Take off the heat and refrigerate
- Microwave when ready to serve
